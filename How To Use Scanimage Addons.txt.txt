All relevant files are contained in Scanimage 3.8.1 addons folder. To use, unzip the folder and add contained files to your scanimage path. For a detailed tutorial on using the software, extract 'Tutorials.zip' and open "Scanimage 3.8.1 Uncaging Automation Tutorial".

Bulk of the addon code written by Michael Smirnov at Max Planck Florida Institute for Neuroscience. Check back for bug fixes and updates.
