All relevant files are contained in Scanimage 3.8.1 folder. To use, unzip the folder and put contained files to your new scanimage path. For a detailed tutorial on using the software, extract 'Tutorials.zip' and open "Scanimage 3.8.1 Uncaging Automation Tutorial".

Bulk of the addon code written by Michael Smirnov and Ryohei Yasuda at Max Planck Florida Institute for Neuroscience. Check back for bug fixes and updates.
